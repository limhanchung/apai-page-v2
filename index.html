Level of concern

The level of concern corresponds to the following values:

  • 0 - not at all concerned
  • 1 - slightly concerned
  • 2 - somewhat concerned
  • 3 - moderately concerned
  • 4 - extremely concerned

Certainty

The less the value of certainty, less weight is given to the range and vice versa
